# 🌾 AGRITECH – Smart Agriculture Assistance System

An AI-powered decision support platform designed to transform Indian agriculture by enabling farmers to make **data-driven, scientific, and profitable decisions**.

---

## 📌 Overview

AGRITECH is a full-stack intelligent farming platform that integrates Machine Learning, weather insights, and market intelligence to assist farmers at every stage of agriculture.

It replaces traditional guesswork with **AI-driven recommendations** to improve yield, reduce risk, and maximize profit.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers in India face multiple challenges:

* Traditional decision-making without data
* Unscientific fertilizer usage damaging soil
* Unpredictable market prices
* Weather uncertainties
* Lack of accessible learning resources

➡️ These issues lead to **low income, crop failure, and financial instability**

---

## 💡 Our Solution

AGRITECH provides an **all-in-one intelligent platform**:

### 🌱 AI Crop Recommendation

* Uses Random Forest Algorithm
* Analyzes soil nutrients and environmental conditions
* Suggests best crop for maximum yield

### 🧪 Smart Fertilizer Recommendation

* Detects nutrient deficiencies
* Calculates optimal NPK ratio
* Improves soil health & productivity

### 📈 Price Prediction

* Powered by CatBoost Algorithm
* Predicts future crop prices
* Helps farmers decide best selling time

### 🌦️ Weather Intelligence

* 7-day weather forecasting
* Helps in irrigation, harvesting, and planning

### 📚 Learning Resources

* Soil care techniques
* Crop rotation strategies
* Pest management guides
